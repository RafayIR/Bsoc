Meet our trainers

Experienced trainers

BSOC has full time trainers who are highly skilled plus highly specialised freelancers have a vast experience in training and consulting. BSOC have advised and consulted on numerous national and international projects.

In-depth education

BSOC team offers a unparalleled range of courses, which even include coffee roasting workshops and trips to the origin. We have daily workshops in German -and regular workshops in English. We do a lot of research on our own.

Center of Berlin

BSOC is based in the very center of Berlin, perfectly located for cousy and modern accomodation, public transport and industry scouting. Coming here and getting around is very easy and we are happy to recommend places.

State of the art

Beautiful spacious training rooms, equipped with the latest state of the art coffee machinery. And lets not even get started on the Green Coffee Library, our portfolio of special coffee nerd equipment and our coffee-bean-shaped table.
